Welcome to Fuse from Kynetx

Fuse is a connected-car application that is designed to give your car a voice and connect it to the rest of your online life.

Fuse data is stored in a personal cloud from Kynetx designed to protect the privacy of that data and put it under your control. To get started you'll have to link Fuse to your personal cloud. You can use the "Authorize" button below to link to an existing Kynetx account or use the "Create" button to create a new account.

Note: please don't sign in with a SquareTag account. Rather, we recommend you create a new account.
